## WEEK ONE EXECUTION PLAN

### Days 1-2

#### Task 1: Finalize Staffing Plan and Roles
- **Owner**: COO
- **Dependency**: None
- **Success Criterion**: Documented staffing plan detailing roles and responsibilities for each team member, including three developers and one project manager, finalized by the end of Day 2.

#### Task 2: Develop Integration Roadmap
- **Owner**: CTO
- **Dependency**: Staffing plan (to understand resource allocation)
- **Success Criterion**: A comprehensive integration roadmap with key milestones, timelines, and resource allocations, presented by the end of Day 2.

#### Task 3: Conduct Market Survey for Enterprise Project Managers
- **Owner**: CMO
- **Dependency**: None
- **Success Criterion**: Draft survey questions focusing on identifying pain points of project managers by the end of Day 2, with a plan for distribution.

### Days 3-4

#### Task 4: Outline MVP Core Features
- **Owner**: CEO
- **Dependency**: Market survey insights (to inform feature selection)
- **Success Criterion**: A prioritized list of core features for the MVP, taking into account user needs and technical feasibility, finalized by the end of Day 4.

#### Task 5: Set Up Weekly Check-ins for Progress Monitoring
- **Owner**: COO
- **Dependency**: None
- **Success Criterion**: A calendar with weekly check-in meetings scheduled and communicated to all stakeholders, finalized by the end of Day 4.

#### Task 6: Establish Daily Stand-ups for Integration Tasks
- **Owner**: CTO
- **Dependency**: Staffing plan (specifically, allocation of one developer to integration)
- **Success Criterion**: Daily stand-up meetings scheduled to monitor integration tasks and facilitate communication, operational by Day 4.

### Days 5-7

#### Task 7: Execute Market Survey
- **Owner**: CMO
- **Dependency**: Completion of survey draft
- **Success Criterion**: Market survey distributed to targeted enterprise project managers by the end of Day 7, with a clear timeline for responses.

#### Task 8: Finalize and Assign Developer Roles
- **Owner**: COO
- **Dependency**: Staffing plan
- **Success Criterion**: Each developer assigned to specific roles and tasks related to the MVP development, confirmed and communicated by Day 7.

#### Task 9: Review Integration Roadmap
- **Owner**: CTO
- **Dependency**: Completion of staffing assignments
- **Success Criterion**: Integration roadmap reviewed and adjusted based on developer assignments, with a clear plan for the first four weeks established by the end of Day 7.

#### Task 10: Prepare for First Weekly Check-in
- **Owner**: COO
- **Dependency**: Completion of initial tasks
- **Success Criterion**: Agenda for the first check-in prepared, including updates on staffing, integration progress, and market survey results, ready for discussion by Day 7.

---

This execution plan is designed to ensure a focused and structured approach to the first week of the project, laying a solid foundation for the 3D Visionary AI Interface's development and timely progression towards the MVP launch. Each task is aimed at addressing key elements critical for success while mitigating potential risks identified earlier.
